# Devaluation-Proof Asset Allocation

This skill focuses on 'Capital Preservation', monitoring global CPI and PPI data to move cash into Gold, Bitcoin, or Land before devaluation occurs.

## Instructions
1. Scrape monthly inflation data from central banks and independent sources.
2. Correlate currency weakness with global commodity price rises.
3. Automatically suggest or execute moves into 'Hard Assets'.
4. Report the 'Real Purchasing Power' of the portfolio, not just the nominal value.

## Examples
- "Move 30% of idle USD into Gold if US inflation crosses 4% year-over-year."
