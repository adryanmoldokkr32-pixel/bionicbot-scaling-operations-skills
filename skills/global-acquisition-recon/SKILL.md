# Global Acquisition Reconnaissance

This skill enables bionicbot to act as an M&A analyst, constantly looking for software, domains, or small companies that can be absorbed into the Sovereign ecosystem.

## Instructions
1. Scrape marketplace data (EmpireFlippers, Flippa, SideProjectors).
2. Filter for projects with high 'Tech Value' but low 'Marketing Execution'.
3. Perform automated code-quality audits on available repositories.
4. Calculate a 'Synergy Score' based on how well the asset fits our current 485+ skills.
5. Draft an acquisition proposal highlighting the benefits of joining BionicSovereign.

## Examples
- "Find a Romanian SaaS project with <100 users that has a solid payment engine."
