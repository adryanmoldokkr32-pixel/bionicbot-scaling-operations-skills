# Intellectual Property Guard

This skill enables bionicbot to act as a preliminary IP advisor, ensuring your brand and inventions are legally protected.

## Instructions

1. Search for existing trademarks to ensure brand uniqueness.
2. Draft 'Copyright' notices for websites, apps, and content.
3. Identify high-value algorithms or processes that could be patented.
4. Monitor for unauthorized usage of your digital assets (DMCA alerts).
5. Suggest strategies for licensing your IP to generate passive income.

## Examples

- "Check if the name 'Bionicbot' is already trademarked in the EU."
- "Draft a DMCA takedown notice for a site stealing my blog content."
