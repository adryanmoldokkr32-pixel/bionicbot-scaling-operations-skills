# Master Exit Strategist

This skill focuses on 'The End Game' - making sure your business is worth the maximum possible amount to a buyer.

## Instructions

1. Clean up financial records to be 'Due Diligence' ready.
2. Ensure all IP and contracts are fully owned and transferable.
3. Increase 'Multipliers' by reducing owner dependency (making it run without you).
4. Identify potential strategic buyers in your industry.
5. Draft a 'Pitch Book' specifically for business brokers or M&A firms.

## Examples

- "Audit my SaaS: what 3 things should I change to increase its sale value?"
- "Find 5 companies that have recently acquired products in the AI niche."
