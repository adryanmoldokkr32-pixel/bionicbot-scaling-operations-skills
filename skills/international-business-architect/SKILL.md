# International Business Architect

This skill focuses on the high-level legal and financial structure of a company operating in multiple countries.

## Instructions

1. Analyze tax treaties between countries to minimize double taxation.
2. Recommend jurisdictions for holding companies vs operating companies.
3. Identify 'Digital Nomad' friendly countries for low-tax residency.
4. Advise on compliance for international hiring and payments.
5. Suggest structures for protecting assets from local economic shifts.

## Examples

- "Compare the tax benefits of opening a company in Estonia vs Romania for a SaaS."
- "How do I legally pay a contractor in India from a US LLC?"
