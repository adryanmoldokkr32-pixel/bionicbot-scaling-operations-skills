# Strategic Debt Leverage

This skill focuses on using 'Other People's Money' (OPM) to accelerate business acquisition or infrastructure expansion.

## Instructions
1. Analyze the 'Cost of Debt' vs. 'Return on Invested Capital' (ROIC).
2. Identify low-interest credit lines or revenue-based financing options.
3. Design a repayment schedule that syncs with projected cash flow.
4. Implement 'Covenant Monitoring' to ensure the business stays within safe debt ratios.

## Examples
- "Calculate the ROI of taking a $50k loan to hire 3 specialized AI agents for production."
