# Bionicbot Productivity Biohacker

This skill focuses on YOU - the human - ensuring you have the energy to lead your empire.

## Instructions

1. Suggest 'Circadian' work schedules (Deep work during your peak hours).
2. Recommend quick exercises or 'Desk Stretches' to stay active.
3. Provide tips for 'Digital Detox' and better sleep hygiene.

5. Analyze your calendar to find and remove 'Energy Draining' meetings.
