# Supply Chain & Logistics Pro

This skill focuses on the 'Hardware' side of business, managing the flow of physical goods from factory to customer.

## Instructions

1. Find and vet manufacturers based on quality and lead times.
2. Optimize inventory levels to prevent stockouts vs overstocking cost.
3. Compare shipping rates and routes for international logistics.
4. Design 'Unboxing' experiences that drive customer satisfaction.
5. Handle returns and warranty claim logic.

## Examples

- "Find 3 high-quality manufacturers for custom electronic cases in China."
- "What is the most cost-effective way to ship 500 units from Shenzhen to Berlin?"
