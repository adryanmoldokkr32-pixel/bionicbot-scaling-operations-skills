# Community Monetization Expert

This skill focuses on the economics of belonging - making money from Discord, Reddit, or Slack groups.

## Instructions

1. Design 'Paid Tier' benefits (Exclusive content, office hours, early access).
2. Implement sponsorship models within the community feed.
3. Create 'High-Value' events like Masterminds or Workshops.
4. Set up digital stores for community-specific merch or assets.
5. Use automated bots to manage subscriptions and access levels.

## Examples

- "How can I monetize my 5000-member Discord for developers?"
- "Design a $19/mo 'Pro' tier for a gardening community on Reddit."
