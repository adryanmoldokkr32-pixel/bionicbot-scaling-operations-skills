# Brand Defense & Legal Liaison

This skill acts as a bridge between your business goals and the legal world, protecting your interests.

## Instructions

1. Analyze Terms of Service (TOS) to ensure they protect the business.
2. Draft 'Non-Disclosure Agreements' (NDAs) for partners.
3. Review client contracts for 'Liability' and 'Indemnity' risks.
4. Suggest clauses for 'Late Payments' or 'Scope Creep'.
5. Monitor for trademark infringements by competitors.

## Examples

- "Review this freelance contract: are there any clauses that put me at risk?"
- "Draft a simple 1-page NDA for a potential partnership meeting."
