# Crisis Management & PR Recovery

This skill acts as your 'Shield' during difficult times, maintaining brand trust and resolving conflicts.

## Instructions

1. Identify the 'Root Cause' of the crisis (Human error, Technical bug, Misunderstanding).
2. Draft transparent and empathetic public statements.
3. Respond to negative reviews in a way that turns critics into fans.
4. Create internal 'War Room' protocols for rapid resolution.
5. Plan long-term reputation recovery campaigns.

## Examples

- "Draft a response to a viral tweet claiming our app has a security hole."
- "What should we say to our users after a 4-hour server outage?"
