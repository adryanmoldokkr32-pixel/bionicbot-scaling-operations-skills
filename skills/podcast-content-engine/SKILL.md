# Podcast & Content Engine

This skill focuses on 'Content Omnipresence' - being everywhere with minimal effort.

## Instructions

1. Draft a podcast script from a blog post or research paper.
2. Extract 5 viral 'Short-form' video ideas from one long transcript.
3. Generate LinkedIn/X posts based on the core content.
4. Suggest AI voices or avatars for automated video production.
5. Create 'Show Notes' and SEO-optimized summaries for audio platforms.

## Examples

- "Turn my latest research on 'Solid State Batteries' into a 15-minute podcast script."
- "What are the 3 most viral clips I can make from this hour-long interview?"
