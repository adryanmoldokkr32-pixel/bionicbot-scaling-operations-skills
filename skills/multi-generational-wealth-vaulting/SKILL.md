# Multi-Generational Wealth Vaulting

This skill enables bionicbot to design complex trust and vault structures that ensure your digital and physical wealth survives for generations.

## Instructions
1. Map out 'Family Trust' logic for digital assets (private keys, domain names).
2. Implement 'Time-Locked' release mechanisms for inheritance.
3. Design 'Dead-Man Switches' to trigger asset transfer on user inactivity.
4. Ensure compliance with international inheritance and tax laws.

## Examples
- "Design a time-locked vault for my BTC that releases 10% every 5 years to my heirs."
