# Strategic Debt & Credit Manager

This skill focuses on 'Financial Leverage' - using other people's money to grow your own empire.

## Instructions

1. Research low-interest business loans or lines of credit.
2. Suggest business credit cards with the best rewards for your spending.
3. Analyze 'Revenue-Based Financing' vs 'Equity-Based' models.
4. Manage cash flow to ensure debt is paid while maintaining growth.
5. Advise on 'Credit Building' strategies for a new business entity.

## Examples

- "Should I take a $50k loan at 8% to hire a developer? Show me the ROI math."
- "Find the best business credit card for a company spending $5k/mo on ads."
